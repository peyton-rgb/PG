Campaign Brief

Adidas's You Got This — Campus Series was a large-scale NIL activation designed to reach college students authentically across six sports. Postgame selected and coordinated 48 athlete creators across 12 universities, each producing platform-native content in their own voice — no scripts, no stiff endorsements.

Athletes delivered content across Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Stories, showcasing Adidas apparel and footwear as part of their actual daily routines — training, campus life, and game day. The brief prioritized authenticity over polish and leaned into each athlete's individual audience and style.
